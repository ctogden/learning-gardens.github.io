Organization

We currently use Slack to communicate and Github to build learning resources. We're avid users of Are.na, which we use to find and collect research materials. In a way, Learning Gardens was born from this network. You're welcome to join and assemble with us.

These platforms were selected for being friendly to free initiatives, and with the exception of Slack, open for the public to view and modify.

Joining

Joining us largely means joining our slack. Say hello!

If you own or participate in your own learning group, we additionally encourage you to message us for further information.
